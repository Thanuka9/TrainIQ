Privacy Policy Agreement

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Version 1.0

Welcome to the TrainIQ Platform (“the System”). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect personal and activity data belonging to employees, administrators, and other authorized users of this platform.

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with standard global data protection frameworks, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as well as applicable industry security standards.

1. Information We Collect

In accordance with data minimization best practices, we may collect and store the following categories of information:

  • Personal Data: Name, official email, employee ID, department, designation, and user role.
  • Authentication Data: Password hashes (bcrypt-encrypted), login logs, and optional 2FA tokens.
  • Usage Data: Task interactions, course progress, exam scores, and dashboard activity.
  • Files & Uploads: Files submitted through the system.
  • Technical Metadata: IP address, device type, browser details (used for security auditing only).

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is processed for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Enable secure, role-based platform access.
  • Assign, manage, and track tasks, training modules, and evaluations.
  • Generate personalized dashboards and administrative reports.
  • Prevent unauthorized access or misuse of the platform.
  • Communicate announcements and task-related notifications.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data on the legal bases of:

  • Operational necessity
  • Legitimate interest
  • User consent, where applicable
  • In alignment with data minimization and purpose limitation guidelines.

4. Data Access and Sharing

  • Access is restricted to authorized administrators based on role and necessity.
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose personal data to third parties.
  • Access logs and audit trails are regularly reviewed for compliance.

5. Document and File Storage

  • Files are securely stored in secure database structures and GridFS.
  • Upload validation (type, size, permissions) is enforced.
  • File access is encrypted in transit and restricted via role-based controls.

6. Data Retention and Removal

In accordance with standard retention policies:

  • Data is retained only for as long as required by operational or HR needs.
  • Upon resignation, role change, or deactivation, data is archived or securely deleted based on compliance protocols and statutory retention requirements.

7. Your Rights

  • Access personal data held.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Request deletion, subject to operational/legal constraints.
  • Object to processing, under specific conditions.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

📧 To exercise any of the above rights, contact: support@trainiq.com

8. Cookies and Session Management

  • Only essential session cookies are used.
  • We do not employ analytics or third-party tracking cookies.

9. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • bcrypt-encrypted password hashing.
  • HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ secure communication.
  • CSRF protection.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
  • Input sanitization to prevent injection and XSS attacks.

10. User Responsibility

  • Maintain confidentiality of credentials.
  • Promptly report any unauthorized access or suspected breaches.
  • Avoid impersonation or system circumvention.

Violations may result in disciplinary action or account suspension.

11. Terms of Use & Exam Rules

By using the System, you agree to the following conduct guidelines:

  • User Identity: Exams must be taken only by the assigned individual.
  • No External Assistance: External tools, notes, or aid are prohibited.
  • Monitoring: Logs including IP, timestamps, and attempts are retained.
  • Integrity: Manipulation of results will lead to invalidation or HR action.
  • Confidentiality: Exam content may not be shared, recorded, or reproduced.

12. Copyright & Intellectual Property

Pursuant to copyright and intellectual property standards:

  • All training materials, exams, documents, and visual content remain the exclusive property of the organizing tenant or TrainIQ.
  • Duplication, reverse engineering, or unauthorized reuse is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary or legal consequences.
