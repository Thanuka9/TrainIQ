Common Issues & Solutions
Issue Suggested Resolution Support Contact
Cannot log in Ensure credentials are correct. Try resetting your password or clearing your browser cache. support@trainiq.io
Tasks not visible Check your role or department assignment. Some tasks are department-specific. Your Department Lead
Study materials not loading Ensure your browser supports inline PDF rendering. Check for access permissions under your role. Education Coordinator
Exam results missing Results may be pending admin review or are available under the "Analytics" section. support@trainiq.io
Incorrect profile data Edit via your profile page. If fields are locked or incorrect, contact the system administrators. System Admins
Cannot invite new users Your organization may have reached its seat limit. Super Admins should go to Admin → Billing to upgrade the plan. Super Admin
Trial expired / cannot log in Free trials last 30 days. After expiry, only Super Admins can sign in to upgrade. Other users should contact their administrator. support@trainiq.com
Invite-only organization Public registration is disabled. Ask your admin for an email invitation link. Super Admin
System Navigation Tips
  • Use the **Sidebar Navigation** to access your Profile, Tasks, Knowledge Base, and Management areas.
  • The **Dashboard** shows active courses, deadlines, and announcements tailored to your role.
  • Toggle **Dark Mode** using the theme icon in the sidebar or top header bar.
  • If your issue persists, please escalate it via the **Support** page or reach out to your supervisor.
